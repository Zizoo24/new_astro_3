## Specialized Medical Translation

Medical translation requires specialized knowledge that goes beyond language proficiency. Our medical translators understand clinical terminology, anatomical terms, and the critical importance of accuracy in healthcare communication.

### Medical Documents We Handle

- **Patient Records**: Medical histories, diagnostic reports, discharge summaries
- **Clinical Research**: Trial protocols, informed consent, research publications
- **Pharmaceutical**: Drug labels, package inserts, regulatory filings
- **Insurance**: Medical claims, coverage documents, policy translations

### Quality Standards

Every medical translation undergoes review by qualified medical translators to ensure terminology accuracy and patient safety compliance.
