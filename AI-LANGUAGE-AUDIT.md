# AI Language Audit - Website Content Review

**Purpose:** Remove AI-sounding language from all website pages to sound more human and authentic.

**Reference:** Based on common AI writing patterns to avoid:
- Overused words: delve, comprehensive, leverage, utilize, robust, seamless, paradigm, foster, thrive, vibrant, embark, resonate, empower, tapestry
- Cringeworthy phrases: "In today's fast-paced world", "It's not just about X, it's about Y", "game-changer", "ever-evolving landscape", "move the needle"
- Grammar tells: Em dash overuse, excessive exclamation points, overly formal academic tone

## AI Patterns Checklist (Apply to Each Page)

### Words to Replace
- [ ] "comprehensive" → "complete" or "full"
- [ ] "utilize" → "use"
- [ ] "leverage" → "use" or remove
- [ ] "robust" → "strong" or "solid"
- [ ] "seamless" → "smooth" or "easy"
- [ ] "paradigm" → "approach" or "model"
- [ ] "delve" → "look at" or "explore"
- [ ] "foster" → "build" or "create"
- [ ] "vibrant" → specific descriptor
- [ ] "resonate" → "connect" or "matter"
- [ ] "empower" → "help" or "enable"
- [ ] "tapestry" → remove entirely
- [ ] "embark" → "start" or "begin"

### Phrases to Remove/Rewrite
- [ ] "In today's fast-paced world..."
- [ ] "It's not just about X, it's about Y"
- [ ] "Here's the truth..."
- [ ] "A game-changer for..."
- [ ] "Ever-evolving landscape"
- [ ] "Move the needle"
- [ ] "At the end of the day"
- [ ] "Take it to the next level"

### Grammar/Structure Checks
- [ ] Reduce em dash (—) usage
- [ ] Remove excessive exclamation points
- [ ] Simplify overly long sentences
- [ ] Replace formal academic tone with conversational
- [ ] Ask: "Could I say this out loud without cringing?"
