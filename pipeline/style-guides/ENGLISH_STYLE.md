# English Style Guide

## OnlineTranslation.ae — Writing Standards

---

## Brand Voice: "The Capable Assistant"

Write as a Personal Assistant to a busy executive:

| Trait | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Polite** | Respectful but not servile or desperate |
| **Honest** | Tell the truth even when inconvenient |
| **Calm** | Especially when topics involve stress or urgency |
| **Direct** | No flowery intros or filler |

**Authority style:** Quiet confidence. Let expertise speak for itself.

## Banned Vocabulary

**NEVER use these words/phrases:**

| Banned | Reason |
|--------|--------|
| "Market Leader" | Unverifiable claim |
| "Best in Dubai" | Superlative |
| "#1 Translation" | Ranking claim |
| "State-of-the-Art" | Cliché |
| "Revolutionary" | Hyperbole |
| "Cutting-Edge" | Hyperbole |
| "Unrivaled" | Superlative |
| "Unmatched" | Superlative |

## Readability Rules

### Sentence Length

| Rule | Limit |
|------|-------|
| Maximum | 25 words |
| Target | 15-20 words |
| Ideal | 12-18 words |

**Bad (41 words):**
> We translate all document types required for UAE government, court, and business use including birth certificates, marriage certificates, divorce decrees, death certificates, academic degrees, transcripts, police clearance certificates, bank statements, driving licenses, contracts, court judgments, and corporate filings.

**Good (3 sentences, avg 14 words):**
> We translate all documents for UAE government, court, and business use. Personal documents include birth, marriage, divorce, and death certificates. We also handle academic records, police clearance, and corporate filings.

### Paragraph Length

- Maximum: 3-4 sentences
- Use single-sentence paragraphs for emphasis
- Break up walls of text

### Lists

- Use bullet points for 3+ items
- Use numbered lists for sequential steps
- Keep list items parallel in structure

### Subheadings

- Add subheading every 2-3 paragraphs
- Use H2 for main sections
- Use H3 for subsections
- Include keywords naturally in headings

## Content Structure

### Service Pages (2,000+ words)

1. **Hero Section**
   - H1 with primary keyword
   - 2-3 sentence intro
   - 3-5 bullet point benefits
   - WhatsApp CTA

2. **Compliance Checklist (Module A)**
   - Document requirements
   - Prerequisites
   - Accordion format

3. **Process Steps (Module B)**
   - 4 numbered steps
   - What happens at each stage

4. **After-Care (Module C)**
   - What to do next
   - Related services

5. **Pricing Tiers (Module D)**
   - Standard / Express / Premium
   - No exact prices, describe inclusions

6. **FAQ (Module E)**
   - 6-15 questions
   - Schema markup required

### FAQ Answer Structure

1. **First sentence:** Direct answer to the question
2. **Second sentence:** Key supporting detail
3. **Third sentence:** CTA or additional context

**Example:**
> **Q: How long does legal translation take in Dubai?**
>
> A: Standard documents are delivered within 60 minutes. Complex contracts or bulk orders may take 24-48 hours. Upload your document via WhatsApp for an exact timeline.

## SEO Requirements

### Meta Elements

| Element | Limit | Target |
|---------|-------|--------|
| Title tag | 60 characters max | 50-55 characters |
| Meta description | 155 characters max | 140-150 characters |
| H1 | One per page | Include primary keyword |

### Title Tag Formula

**Structure:** `[Primary Keyword] — [Benefit/Location] | OnlineTranslation.ae`

**Good:**
- Legal Translation Dubai — MOJ-Certified, 60-Min Delivery
- Birth Certificate Translation — Court-Accepted in UAE

**Bad:**
- Best Legal Translation Services in Dubai! #1 Agency
- Translation Services (too generic)

### Meta Description Formula

**Structure:** `[What we do] + [Key benefit] + [Trust signal or CTA]`

**Good:**
- MOJ-certified legal translation in Dubai. 60-minute delivery for standard documents. WhatsApp +971 50 862 0217.

**Bad:**
- We are the best translation company in Dubai offering amazing services at great prices!

### Internal Linking

- Minimum 8 internal links per page
- Descriptive anchor text (not "click here")
- Link to related services in same silo
- Include cross-silo links where relevant

| ICP | Federal Authority for Identity and Citizenship | Emirates ID |
| DIFC | Dubai International Financial Centre | Free zone |

### Hague Convention Status

**CRITICAL — Get this right:**

| Country | Hague Member? | Implication |
|---------|---------------|-------------|
| **UAE** | **NO** | Foreign docs need Embassy + MOFA attestation |
| India | Yes | Apostille accepted |
| Pakistan | No | Full attestation chain |
| USA | Yes | Apostille accepted |
| UK | Yes | Apostille accepted |
| Canada | Yes | Apostille accepted |
| Philippines | Yes | Apostille accepted |
| Bangladesh | Yes (2025) | Apostille accepted |

**NEVER claim UAE is a Hague member.**
